# 🏠 FamilyHub — Tester Roadmap & Guide

**Welcome to FamilyHub!** We're building a private, family-friendly platform to help families stay connected, organized, and entertained together. This document explains what's live now, what's coming next, and how you can help shape the product.

---

## 📍 What's Live Right Now (Phase 1 — MVP)

FamilyHub is **live and ready to use** at: **https://familyhub-d562.pre.dev**

### Core Features You Can Use Today

#### 💬 **Messaging & Communication**
- Send messages to your family in a shared message board
- Reply to specific messages (threaded conversations)
- Use emojis and reactions to express yourself
- See who's online and typing in real-time

#### 🖼️ **Photo & Video Gallery**
- Upload and share family photos and videos
- Organize media in a shared gallery
- View a timeline of family moments
- Download photos to your device

#### 📆 **Shared Calendar**
- Create and view family events
- See birthdays, anniversaries, and important dates
- Coordinate family gatherings
- Get reminders for upcoming events

#### 🛒 **Shopping Lists**
- Create shared shopping lists (groceries, errands, etc.)
- Assign items to family members
- Check off items as you shop
- Organize by categories (produce, dairy, household, etc.)

#### 🎥 **Video Calls**
- Make two-way video calls with family members
- Crystal-clear audio and video
- Perfect for face-to-face conversations

#### 🎬 **Streaming Theater**
- Watch free movies and TV shows together
- Browse content from Pluto TV, Tubi, Roku, and Freeview
- Family-friendly entertainment

#### 👤 **Profiles & Settings**
- Create your family profile with your name and photo
- Manage your account settings
- Control your privacy and notification preferences
- Choose your language (English, Spanish, French, and more)

#### 💝 **Donations & Support**
- Support causes you care about
- Make secure donations through the app
- See your donation history

## 🐛 How to Report Bugs

Found something that doesn't work? **Please tell us!** Your feedback is invaluable.

### Report a Bug

1. **What happened?** Describe what you were trying to do
2. **What went wrong?** What did you see instead?
3. **How can we reproduce it?** Step-by-step instructions
4. **What device/browser?** (e.g., iPhone Safari, Chrome on Windows)
5. **Screenshot?** If possible, include a screenshot

**Send bug reports to:** [Your email or contact method]

### Example Bug Report

> **Title:** Photos not uploading from mobile
> 
> **What I was doing:** Trying to upload a photo from my iPhone camera roll
> 
> **What happened:** The upload button didn't respond when I clicked it

> 2. Click "Upload Photo"

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Is FamilyHub free?

**Yes!** The core features (messaging, gallery, calendar, shopping lists, video calls, streaming) are completely free. Premium features (AI photo tagging, photo digitization) will be optional paid add-ons.

### Is my data private?

**Absolutely.** FamilyHub is private — only your family members can see your data. We don't sell your information or show ads. Your data is encrypted and secure.

### Can I invite more family members?

**Yes!** Ask your family admin for an invite link. They can add as many family members as you want.

### What if I find a security issue?

**Please tell us immediately.** Don't post it publicly. Email us at [Your security contact] with details.

### Can I delete my account?

**Yes.** Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed.

### What languages does FamilyHub support?

**Many!** The app automatically detects your browser language. Currently supported: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and more. Let us know if you'd like another language added.
