## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC BEST PRACTICES

### Twitter/X

**Optimal Posting Times**:
- Weekdays: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- Weekends: 9-10 AM, 7-9 PM

**Content Mix**:
- 40% Feature spotlights & tips
- 25% Engagement & questions
- 20% Testimonials & user stories
- 15% Industry insights & thought leadership

**Best Practices**:
- Keep tweets concise (under 280 characters when possible)
- Use 1-2 relevant hashtags per tweet
- Include visuals (images/videos increase engagement by 150%)
- Ask questions to encourage replies
- Retweet and engage with relevant accounts
- Use threads for longer-form content
- Post consistently (3-5 times daily)
- Respond to mentions within 2 hours

**Hashtag Strategy**:
- Primary: #FamilyHub, #FamilyConnection, #FamilyFirst
- Secondary: #FamilyTech, #StayConnected, #MultiGenerational
- Trending: Monitor daily and use relevant trends
- Avoid: More than 2 hashtags per tweet

**Engagement Goals**:
- Target: 2-5% engagement rate
- Retweets: 10-20% of impressions
- Replies: 5-10% of impressions
- Likes: 50-100% of impressions

### Instagram

**Optimal Posting Times**:
- Weekdays: 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- Weekends: 10-11 AM, 6-8 PM

**Content Mix**:
- 35% Feed posts (stories, features, testimonials)
- 30% Reels (demos, tips, behind-the-scenes)
- 20% Stories (daily updates, polls, Q&A)
- 15% Carousel posts (how-tos, comparisons)

**Best Practices**:
- Post 1-2 feed posts per day
- Post 3-5 stories daily
- Post 2-3 reels per week
- Use captions to tell stories (150-300 characters)
- Include 5-10 relevant hashtags
- Use location tags
- Engage with followers' content daily
- Respond to comments within 1 hour
- Use Instagram Stories for real-time updates
- Create Highlights for important content

**Hashtag Strategy**:
- Mix popular (1M+ posts) and niche (10K-100K posts) hashtags
- Create branded hashtag (#FamilyHubMoments)
- Use 8-10 hashtags per post
- Research hashtags before posting

**Engagement Goals**:
- Target: 3-5% engagement rate
- Likes: 100-500 per post
- Comments: 10-50 per post
- Saves: 20-100 per post
- Shares: 5-20 per post

**Reel Strategy**:
- Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
- Use trending audio
- Include text overlays
- End with clear CTA
- Post 2-3 reels per week
- Target: 10K-50K views per reel

- Use professional tone

**Hashtag Strategy**:
- Primary: #FamilyHub, #FamilyConnection, #Technology
- Industry: #FamilyTech, #Innovation, #SaaS
- Trending: Monitor LinkedIn trending topics

**Engagement Goals**:
- Target: 2-4% engagement rate
- Likes: 50-200 per post
- Comments: 10-30 per post
- Shares: 5-15 per post

**Article Strategy**:
- Publish 1-2 long-form articles per month

- Include data/research
- Add visuals
- Promote via other channels

## ENGAGEMENT & COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

### Response Time Guidelines

| Message Type | Response Time | Owner |
|-------------|---------------|-------|
| Direct Messages | Within 1 hour | Community Manager |
| Comments | Within 2 hours | Community Manager |
| Mentions | Within 2 hours | Community Manager |
| Support Questions | Within 4 hours | Support Team |
| Complaints/Issues | Within 1 hour | Manager |

### Comment Response Templates

**Positive Comment**:
```
"Thank you so much for the kind words! 💙 We're so glad you're enjoying FamilyHub. 
Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or feature requests!"
```

**Question About Features**:
```
"Great question! [Answer]. If you'd like to learn more, check out our help center: [link]. 
Feel free to DM us if you need additional support!"
```

**Feature Request**:
```
"We love the idea! Feature requests like yours help us improve FamilyHub. 
You can submit feature requests in our community forum: [link]. Thanks for the feedback!"
```

**Complaint/Issue**:
```
"We're sorry to hear you're experiencing this issue. We'd love to help! 
Please DM us with more details, and our support team will get back to you within 1 hour."
```

**Competitor Mention**:
```
"Thanks for bringing this up! We think FamilyHub stands out because [unique value]. 
We'd love to show you how we're different. Feel free to DM us for a demo!"
```

### Community Building Tactics

**Daily**:
- Respond to all comments and DMs
- Engage with 10-20 relevant posts from other accounts

- Share user-generated content

**Weekly**:

**Monthly**:

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT & BRAND SAFETY

### Crisis Response Protocol

**Step 1: Assess**
- Identify the issue
- Determine severity (low/medium/high)
- Gather all relevant information
- Check if it's trending

**Step 2: Respond**
- Acknowledge the issue within 1 hour
- Show empathy and understanding
- Provide solution or next steps
- Avoid being defensive

**Step 3: Follow Up**
- Ensure issue is resolved
- Thank person for bringing it up
- Share learnings with team
- Update policies if needed

### Crisis Response Templates

**Service Issue**:
```
"We're aware of the issue and sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. 
Our team is working on a fix and we'll have an update within [timeframe]. 
Thank you for your patience and please DM us if you need immediate assistance."
```

**Negative Review/Feedback**:
```
"Thank you for the feedback. We're sorry to hear about your experience. 
We'd love to make this right. Please DM us with more details and we'll follow up within 1 hour."
```

**Misinformation**:
```
"We want to clarify: [Correct information]. 
If you have questions, please check our help center [link] or DM us. Thanks for giving us a chance to explain!"
```

**Competitor Attack**:
```
"We appreciate the comparison. We think FamilyHub stands out because [unique value]. 
We'd love to show you the difference. Feel free to DM us for a demo!"
```

### Content Safety Guidelines

**Do NOT Post**:
- Controversial political content
- Religious content (unless brand-relevant)
- Graphic violence or adult content
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Misinformation or false claims
- Competitor attacks
- Spam or promotional content

**Do Post**:
- Family-friendly content
- Inclusive and diverse representation
- Positive, uplifting messages
- Educational and helpful content
- Community stories and testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes authentic moments

### Moderation Policy

**Comment Moderation**:
- Delete spam and promotional comments
- Delete hate speech and discrimination
- Delete misinformation
- Delete off-topic comments (optional)
- Keep constructive criticism and feedback

**Account Blocking**:
- Block accounts that repeatedly violate guidelines
- Block spam accounts
- Block accounts promoting harmful content
- Document reasons for blocking
